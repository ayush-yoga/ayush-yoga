About

Rooted in tradition,
present with you

Hi, I'm Nandini. My yoga journey began as a teenager in Mysuru, India — the spiritual heartland of Ashtanga yoga — where the practice wove itself into the rhythm of daily life. After moving to the United States, I deepened my formal training and earned my teaching certification at Life Time Power Yoga.

I continue my learning under Greg Tebb in the authentic Manju Jois lineage, staying true to the classical Ashtanga tradition passed down directly from Pattabhi Jois. I am also a certified Ayurveda Counselor, and I weave Ayurvedic principles into my teaching to offer a genuinely holistic wellness experience.

Over 15 years and 500+ students later, I teach weekly classes at Gym Studios and YMCA locations across Austin, TX — and offer private sessions and personalized Ayurvedic consultations for those seeking deeper, one-on-one guidance.

What I offer

Authentic Ashtanga yoga in the Manju Jois tradition, complementary class styles for all needs, and certified Ayurvedic counseling — all offered across Austin, TX.

Ashtanga Yoga

Classical Ashtanga yoga taught in the authentic Manju Jois lineage — a dynamic, breath-centered practice that builds strength, flexibility, and meditative focus.

  • Ashtanga Primary Series
  • Mysore-style Self-Practice
  • Led Ashtanga Classes
  • Pranayama & Meditation
  • Private 1-on-1 Sessions

Ayurveda Counseling

As a certified Ayurveda Counselor, I offer in-depth consultations to assess your constitution (prakriti) and create a personalized plan for diet, lifestyle, and daily routines.

  • Prakriti (Constitution) Assessment
  • Personalized Diet & Nutrition Plans
  • Dinacharya (Daily Routine) Guidance
  • Herbal & Lifestyle Recommendations
  • Seasonal Wellness Programs
